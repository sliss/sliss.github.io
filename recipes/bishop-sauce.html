Bishop Sauce

Ingredients

~32oz tomato sauce (sauce, not paste)


1 bottle molasses (I forget oz, but it's only sold in one size usually


~1-2 cups of Apple Cider Vinegar, depending on desired vinegar-y-ness. I always forget the exact ratio here and end up fudging it with more-as-needed while it's simmering.


Other sweetener to taste (honey, sugar, apple butter, lots of options here that probably don't make THAT much difference, except for a touch of citrus ends up being nice, I think. Probably the best batch I've ever made used winter-seasonal hard cider.)


Spice blend in approximately decreasing order of quantity:


3 Brown Sugar


3 (Smoked) Paprika


1 Garlic powder


1 Onion powder


Cayenne powder (dash)


Chili Powder (dash)


Garlic salt or sea salt


Black Pepper


Dried mustard


Cumin (can be omitted, use a very light touch here)


a few drops of Liquid Smoke

Description

The good sauce

Directions

Throw all liquids in a pot, dump spices, simmer on low-med heat stirring FREQUENTLY if not constantly for 1-2 hours
